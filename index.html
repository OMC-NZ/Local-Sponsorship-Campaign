OPPO, in partnership with Spark New Zealand, are proud sponsors of the Wellington Phoenix, New Zealand’s only professional football club. This unique three-way partnership aims to bring football and technology together through innovation and creativity while simultaneously supporting these talented football players in all their success.

PLAYERS TO WATCH OUT FOR

ALEX RUFER

Alex Rufer is a 25-year-old kiwi-born midfielder, who is entering his ninth season with the Wellington Phoenix and has made an impressive 88 club appearances for the Phoenix. Rufer is 184cm tall and weighs 75kg. Alex is certainly a player to keep your eye on.

GARY HOOPER

Gary Hooper is a 33-year-old English-born forward who is 177cm tall. Hooper will be returning to the Wellington Phoenix after having played for the team in 2019/2020.

TIM PAYNE

Tim Payne is a 27-year-old kiwi-born defender, who is entering his third seasotn with the Wellington Phoenix. Payne is 179cm tall and weighs 73kg.

BEN WAINE

Ben Waine is a 19-year-old kiwi-born forward entering his third season with the Wellington Phoenix. Waine is 179cm tall.

SUCCEED LIKE THE PHOENIX WITH OPPO

OPPO products will be assisting the Phoenix in achieving their health, fitness and career goals over the coming years. Our players live busy lives, but with the help of the OPPO ecosystem, we’re ensuring that they don’t have to worry about manually tracking their progress or missing a single moment. The OPPO Watch is the ideal training buddy for our high-performing athletes who enjoy listening to Spotify offline using the OPPO Enco audio range while on a run, simultaneously tracking their fitness and heart rate and grabbing a coffee using Google pay on the way home. With our Find X3 series, the coaches catch high-definition shots of the players training. Using the dual 50mp primary cameras to capture crystal clear images and videos, the players can review their efforts and improve their game on the billion colour display. Check out more of our OPPO products to see how we can help you succeed like the Wellington Phoenix.
